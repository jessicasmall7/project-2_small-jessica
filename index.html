About the App: Gym Buddy

Gym Buddy is an app that allows gyms/fitness centers to connect to fitness lovers. Gyms make a profile and can upload their hours, class times, personal training availability, staff bios, available jobs, and more. Then individuals make profiles and can follow the gyms they frequent, easily signing up for classes, personal training sessions, and paying their membership.

This accommodates people who might seek different facilities for different specialties – think yoga studio, spin studio, seasonal boot camps, and regular membership. If a facility has a café or nutritional element, you can also order food via the app, so that its prepared while you work out, and ready when you are.
